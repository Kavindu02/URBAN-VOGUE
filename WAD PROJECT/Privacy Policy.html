Privacy Policy

We collect the following information when you create an account or make a purchase:

  • Personal Details: Your name, email address, and contact information.

  • Payment Information: Securely processed for transactions.

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To fulfill your purchases and deliver products.
  • Improving Services: Personalization helps enhance your shopping experience.
  • Communication: We may contact you regarding orders, promotions, or updates.

3. Security Measures

Your data is protected through:

  • Encryption: Urban Vogue employs industry-standard encryption protocols.
  • Access Control: Unauthorized access is prevented through strict controls.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Review and update your personal information.
  • Correction: Correct any inaccuracies in your data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated data.

For assistance, please reach out to our customer support team.

5. Policy Updates

Our privacy policy may change over time. Please check our website for the latest version.
